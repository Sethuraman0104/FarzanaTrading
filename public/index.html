About Farzana Trading

Farzana Trading is a Bahrain-based supplier of construction materials, pallets, electrical cables and scrap management services. Since our establishment, we have committed to timely deliveries, transparent pricing and long-term relationships with industrial and construction clients.

Vision

To be the region's most trusted partner for sustainable supply-chain solutions — bridging quality materials with efficient logistics.

Mission

  • Provide high-quality materials and accessories at competitive prices.
  • Offer dependable scrap collection & resale services that reduce waste.
  • Build lasting partnerships with clients through transparency and reliability.

Services

  • Bulk supply of building materials (cement, steel, bricks, aggregates)
  • Pallet supply — wooden & plastic; custom sizes & quantities
  • Electrical cables & wiring — multiple mm specifications
  • Scrap collection, valuation and buyer network access
  • Logistics coordination & delivery across Bahrain

Fast quotes · Verified clients · Dedicated account managers · Competitive pricing
